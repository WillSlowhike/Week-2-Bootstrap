Maine Coon

Known for their large size and friendly personalities, Maine Coons are often called "gentle giants."

Siamese

Siamese cats are vocal, social, and have striking blue eyes and a sleek coat.

Persian

Persian cats are known for their long, luxurious fur and calm, gentle demeanor.

Bengal

Bengal cats are energetic, playful, and have a wild, leopard-like appearance.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are affectionate, relaxed, and known for going limp when picked up.

Sphynx

Sphynx cats are hairless, curious, and love to be the center of attention.
